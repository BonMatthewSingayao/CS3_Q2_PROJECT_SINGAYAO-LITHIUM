THE MAZE RUNNER
AUTHOR: JAMES DASHNER
The dystopian novel follows Thomas, a sixteen year old who wakes up in a maze with no memory of who he is. He joins the other people present in the maze and work together to make a peaceful community.
However, they are attacked by mysterious robotic monsters and try to escape the maze. Throughout the novel, they discovery secrets and why they were in the maze as they escape.

THE SCORCH TRIALS
AUTHOR: JAMES DASHNER
The Scorch Trials is the second book in the "Maze Runner" series. It now follows Thomas and his friends who escaped the maze and realize that it was the doing of an organization named WCKD.
They search for clues about WCKD in a barren and dangerous wasteland caled the Scorch. Along the way, they face many challenges, new people, new enemies, and betrayals.

THE DEATH CURE
AUTHOR: JAMES DASHNER
This book is the third installment in the "Maze Runner" series. Thomas and his friends discover that there is a disease called the flare that turns people into mindless zombies.
They try and save one another from this disease by breaking in WCKD departments to find the cure. Here, many secrets were revealed and the friends felt betrayed. They faced many opponents throughout.
