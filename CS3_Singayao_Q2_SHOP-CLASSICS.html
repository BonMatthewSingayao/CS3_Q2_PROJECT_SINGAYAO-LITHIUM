SILAS MARNER
AUTHOR: GEORGE ELIOT
The novel tells the story of Silas, who was falsely accused of stealing funds. He was framed for a crime he did not commit.
Silas believes in faith and that it will protect him but when God doesn't protect him, he abandons his religion.

TRISTRAM SHANDY
AUTHOR: LAURENCE STERNE
The novel narrates the life and opinions of the main character and his family. Though it has no plot, action, and heroine.
It is full of stories within stories, digressions, and experiences instead, covering the years 1680 - 1766.
CLASSICS | LITERATURE | BRITISH LITERATURE | FICTION | 18TH CENTURY
MANSFIELD PARK
AUTHOR: JANE AUSTEN
The novel shows the story of Fanny Price who was sent to live to the household of the wealthy instead of her overburdened family.
Jane Austen's views about religion, slavery, theatrical performances are also shown in the story.
